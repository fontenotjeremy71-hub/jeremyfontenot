Evidence Boundary

What this lab does not prove.

The evidence does not claim enterprise scale, production availability, recurring restore assurance, RTO/RPO, full disaster recovery, full firewall assurance, broad security assurance, SLA performance, or employer-system administration. Timeout-handling tests do not prove service availability, and drift gaps do not by themselves prove infrastructure failure.
