Limitations

What the evidence does not prove

The lab is personal and nonproduction. Backup archive presence does not prove an application-level restore. Snapshots are not independent backups. TCP reachability is not complete service validation. Retained isolated restore startup checks do not establish recurring DR assurance, RTO, or RPO. DC01 is the only domain controller.
